Our Ministries

Serving God and the community through prayer, fellowship, and outreach.

Sunday School

Enlightening our children about Christianity and moral life. Classes are held every Sunday in Malayalam. Annual anniversaries include songs, dances, skits, and Vacation Bible School.

St. Mary’s Youth Association (SMYANs)

Actively working for the growth of the church through youth fellowship and charity. Organizes carols, camps, and supports needy students while nurturing leadership and service.

Martha Mariam Vanitha Samajam

A fellowship for women, enhancing spiritual and social growth. Members lead charitable works, support families, and promote Christian values in everyday life.

Church Choir

Inspiring worship through hymns and songs. The choir actively participates in weekly services, weddings, and festivals, uplifting the congregation in faith.
